About Me

As an ex-FBI feild agent, I found life a bit boring always beging away from home seeing the great wonders of our nation, always seeing the worst and best of people, being able to go to crime scene investigation.

Top secret missions, constant rigerous training, watching my pensions, 401ks, stocks, bonds grow to numbers that shouldn't exist, and the like was something that needed to change, I had to do something different in my life, I wanted to stay put for a change.

Life was just too easy. I had to do something else, so I decided to pack up my family without a clear vision, go put my skills to a real test. I applied to a six month boot-camp at UT Austin where I would be grounded and be pushed mentally in ways the FBI could never do!
